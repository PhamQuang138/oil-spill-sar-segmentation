LUONG HOAT DONG STREAMLIT APP

1. Khoi dong app
   - Lenh chay: streamlit run app/streamlit_app.py
   - app/streamlit_app.py them thu muc goc project vao sys.path de import duoc src.

2. Chon device
   - Sidebar cho chon Auto, CPU hoac CUDA.
   - Auto se dung CUDA neu torch.cuda.is_available(), nguoc lai dung CPU.

3. Load model
   - Ham load_models(device) duoc cache bang st.cache_resource de tranh load lai model nhieu lan.
   - App tim weight Quang tai weights/quang_best_deeplabv3plus_checkpoint.pth.
   - App tim weight Hung tai weights/Hung_best_segformer_checkpoint.pth.
   - App tim weight Khoa tai weights/Khoa_best_Unetplusplus_checkpoint.pth.
   - Neu load duoc it nhat 2 model, app tao them EnsemblePredictor de lay trung binh probability map.

4. Upload anh
   - Nguoi dung upload anh SAR dinh dang png, jpg, jpeg, tif hoac tiff.
   - Anh duoc doc bang PIL, convert sang RGB, roi chuyen thanh numpy array.

5. Chay du doan
   - Khi bam nut Chay Nhan Dien, app lay predictor theo model da chon.
   - predictor.predict(image_np, threshold) tra ve:
     + prob_map: ban do xac suat vet dau loang, gia tri 0..1.
     + mask: mat na nhi phan, pixel dau = 1, nen = 0.

6. Tao overlay
   - BasePredictor.generate_overlay(image_np, mask, alpha) resize anh goc ve cung kich thuoc mask.
   - Cac pixel mask = 1 duoc to mau do va blend voi anh goc theo alpha.

7. Hien thi ket qua
   - App tinh ti le dau: mask.mean() * 100.
   - Man hinh hien 3 cot:
     + Anh goc.
     + Mask du doan.
     + Anh overlay.

8. Ghi chu hien tai
   - App hien co QuangPredictor, HungPredictor va KhoaPredictor.
   - Ensemble hien chi duoc tao khi co tu 2 model tro len.
